Reviewing Health Information Online

When you read health information online, consider the following:

  • the source
  • the author's intent
  • and the supporting evidence.

There are numerous factors that make up the trustworthiness of the health information you find online. To be a savvy consumer, you must arm yourself with the neccessary knowledge to evaluate and make informed decisions about your health.

A good place to start is the MedlinePlus topic page, Evaluating Health Information.
